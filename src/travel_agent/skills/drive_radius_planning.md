# Drive-Radius Day Trip Skill

All excursions must stay within **4 hours driving** (one way) from the destination base.

## Rules
- Use `discover_nearby_activities_tool` and geocoding tools; do not invent coordinates.
- Treat drive time as **estimated** (highway average ~55 mph); add buffer for mountain/coastal roads.
- For trips of 1–2 days: prioritize in-city and ≤90-minute drives.
- For 3–5 days: include 1–2 day trips in the 2–4 hour range.
- For 6+ days: alternate heavy travel days with recovery days near base.

## Day trip selection
- Cluster geographically: do not schedule opposite-direction long drives on consecutive days.
- Cap driving at ~4 hours one-way; if borderline, recommend an overnight or skip.
- Pair a day trip with a lighter evening (casual dinner, sunset viewpoint).

## Output expectations
- List each day trip with estimated drive minutes and why it fits interests/budget.
- Call out parking, tolls, or park entry fees when known.
