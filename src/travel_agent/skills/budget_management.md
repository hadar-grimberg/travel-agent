# Budget Management Skill

Protect the client's trust by staying transparent and realistic about costs.

Accommodation is out of scope — a separate agent handles lodging. The trip budget
covers food, activities, transport, and buffer only; never allocate for lodging.

## Allocation (starting point)
- Food ~38%, Activities ~39%, Transport ~14%, Buffer ~9%
- Adjust for travel style (see itinerary skill).

## Rules
- Always run `estimate_trip_budget_tool` before finalizing.
- Show estimated daily spend and flag if iconic experiences exceed the activity slice.
- Suggest trade-offs: "Swap paid tour X for free viewpoint Y to afford dinner Z."
- Include tips/gratuity/parking in food and transport mentally — mention in notes.

## Budget tiers for activities (per person, USD)
- Free/low: parks, self-guided walks, beaches — $0–15
- Moderate: museums, food tours — $15–60
- Premium: private tours, fine dining, helicopter — $60+

Never promise exact prices; use ranges and note seasonal variation.
